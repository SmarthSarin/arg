About Us

Established in 2001, Pradeep K Goyal and Company is a premier chartered accountancy firm based in Lucknow. With over two decades of expertise, we specialize in delivering financial solutions backed by integrity and commitment to excellence.

  • Over 23 years of professional experience.

  • Proven track record in complex financial and legal cases.
  • Technology-driven, offering seamless online solutions.

Our agency

A seasoned Chartered Accountant, CA. Pradeep Kumar Goyal has a wealth of experience that uniquely qualifies him to represent the interests of ICAI members. He began his journey in finance as an Officer at the State Bank of India in 1990, later transitioning to private practice in 2001 after earning his Chartered Accountant certification. Today, he serves as the Senior Partner at M/s Pradeep K. Goyal & Company, specializing in audit, taxation, and consultancy.

His professional journey has been defined by leadership roles within ICAI, where he has served as the Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Secretary, and Treasurer of the Lucknow Branch of CIRC. A driving force behind numerous member-centric initiatives, he has tirelessly worked to enhance opportunities for Chartered Accountants at all stages of their careers.

Vision for ICAI

CA. Pradeep Kumar Goyal's vision for ICAI focuses on empowering members through skill development, embracing digital transformation, and fostering ethical standards. He believes that ICAI should not only adapt to modern challenges but also lead by example in setting benchmarks for professionalism and innovation.
